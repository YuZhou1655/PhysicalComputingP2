Weather Boxx

CIM383 Physical Computing Project 2 Life Hack

What can this Weather Box do?

Pattern are created with generative AI in Adobe Illustrator

This weather box is designed to help you indentify the weather and provide some emergency light in dark. It comes with a light sensor, on the one hand, it will detects the weather based the light it received, on the other hand, it will turns the light on itself when the enviroment gets too dark

How it works?

On the sides of Weather Box, there is one port on each side for button acess and wire connection. After you plug in the cable, if you hear some noise from the motor, then it is ready. You can press the button from the port on the other side to activate the Weather Box.

Once it is activated, the motor will spin to certain degree based on how much light it receives. Then you can know the current weather condition from the display port on the top of the Weather Box.

Issues and Solutions

Issue 1 : Too Large

The first draft had each part sitting in its own space which makes the whole weather box a 6 inch sub. It going to takes hours to print all the holders, and rollers I need for the weather box. On the other hand, this structur leaves to many empty space inside.

Solution : Weather Box Slim

Instead of having the button on top, it is now on the board. Instead of having breadboard and the circuit playground side by side, I put the circuit playground on the board.

Issue 2 : Spaghetti

At first, I wanted 3d print out the box. After a few failure attempt due to "spaghetti", I noticed it is more likely to have issues when printing surface.

Solution : Use paperboards instend of 3D printing

How hard can a box be? Instead of 3D print out the whole box, I use cricut to cut out the paper boards for the box. Only use 3D printer for the corners.
